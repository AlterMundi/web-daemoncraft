## 🎨 Paleta de Colores (NUEVA)

### Primarios
- **Dark Navy:** `#0A1428` — Fondo oscuro, soberanía
- **Daemon Cyan:** `#00D9FF` — Vida, presencia del daemon
- **Gold Accent:** `#FFB800` — Importancia, CTAs, adventura
- **Deep Purple:** `#2D1B4E` — Misterio, profundidad

### Secundarios
- **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF` — Textos claros
- **Light Gray:** `#E8E8E8` — Inputs, backgrounds suaves
- **Success Green:** `#10B981` — Confirmaciones, "Sumate"
- **Danger Red:** `#EF4444` — Alertas

### Gradientes
- **Hero Gradient:** Dark Navy → Deep Purple (45deg)

## 🎬 Visual Style

### Layout
- **Max-width:** 1280px
- **Padding:** 60px horizontal, 80px vertical
- **Grid:** 12 columnas

### Cards
- **Border:** 2px solid Daemon Cyan (glow effect)
- **Background:** Dark Navy con gradient overlay
- **Hover:** Cyan glow aumenta, transform: translateY(-4px)
- **Radius:** 16px

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Gold background, Dark Navy text, hover: Cyan glow
- **Secondary:** Cyan border, transparent bg, hover: Cyan fill
- **Size:** 48px height (accessible)

### Inputs
- **Background:** Light Gray
- **Border:** 1px Daemon Cyan
- **Focus:** 2px Daemon Cyan border, Cyan glow
- **Placeholder:** Dim gray

### Animations
- **Entrance:** fade + slide up (0.6s, ease-out)
- **Hover:** glow + lift (0.3s, ease-out)
- **Scroll:** reveal + parallax (continuous)
